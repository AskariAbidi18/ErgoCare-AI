## Purpose
This document defines ethical data handling rules for the ErgoCare AI system.

---

## Data Collection Principles
- Only collect data required for ergonomic assessment
- Collect explicit consent before questionnaire submission
- Store anonymized faculty IDs only
- Do not store names, phone numbers, emails, or addresses

---

## Data Storage Rules
- Store raw questionnaire responses separately from processed datasets
- Restrict access to raw datasets
- Maintain logs for dataset access

---

## Data Retention
- Raw responses should be retained only as long as necessary for analysis
- Aggregated anonymized results may be retained for longitudinal insights

---

## No Re-identification
The system must never attempt to infer identity from:
- department details
- age bands
- unique combinations of answers

---

## Secure Handling
- datasets must be encrypted at rest when stored in production
- backups must be protected

---

## Reporting Rules
Reports must not contain any personally identifying information.
